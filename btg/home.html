Mission Statement

Empowering students from JK to Grade 12 to unlock their full potential through personalized, flexible, affordable online tutoring sessions.

Website Owner Profile

Bridge the Gap Tutoring is founded by 4 passionate educators who have supported students all accross Canada, enabling them to reach new heights in academia. We emphasise measurable success and growth, while also considering the numerous barriers to education. We believe a quality education should be accessible to all students, and thus, we bridge the gap to success.
